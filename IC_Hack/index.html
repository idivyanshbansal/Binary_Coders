ZEN

An automated platform that mentors may use to keep track of their mentees' progress while also honing their self-learning abilities on various coding and learning platforms. One of the features gives the mentor admin rights and includes a dashboard for both the mentor and the mentee. A notification panel and weekly reports keep the mentor and mentees informed of students' performance levels. A leadership chart that the students may use to assess their own performance. For the mentor and mentee to communicate and cover networking, there will also be a chat and video conferencing option.  Along with that, we have come up with the idea of integrating block coding for the students to work on getting better at logical thinking and problem-solving.
